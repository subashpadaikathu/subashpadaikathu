EASWARI ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Civil Services

Civil Service

Empowering Future Leaders of the Nation

Easwari Engineering College proudly offers a comprehensive Civil Services Training Program aimed at preparing students for one of India’s most prestigious and competitive career paths – the Indian Civil Services (IAS, IPS, IFS, etc.). Our program is designed to nurture ambition, instill discipline, and deliver the skills required to succeed in the UPSC Civil Services Examination.

Why Choose Us?

Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced mentors, including IAS officers, subject matter experts, and UPSC veterans.

Structured Curriculum:Aligned with the latest UPSC syllabus – covering Prelims, Mains, and Interview stages.

Integrated Learning:Seamlessly balances engineering education with civil service preparation.

Mentorship and Guidance:Personalized mentorship programs for continuous progress tracking and motivation.

Mock Tests & Evaluations: Regular practice exams with performance feedback to build exam temperament and accuracy.

    Training Mode

  • Hybrid Classes: Online and offline sessions
  • Weekend & Evening Batches to suit academic schedules
  • Guest Lectures by successful UPSC candidates and serving civil servants

    Outcomes

  • Strong foundation for cracking the UPSC CSE
  • Enhanced analytical, communication, and leadership skills
  • Greater awareness of national and international issues
  • A clear roadmap to government careers beyond engineering

Join the Mission Shape the Nation.

Take the first step toward a career in public service with Easwari Engineering College’s Civil Services Training Program. We are committed to guiding you every step of the way in your journey toward the UPSC.
